Employee Management System

Efficiently manage your workforce with our Employee Management System, designed to streamline employee data handling and enhance organizational productivity.

  • ✅ Employee Details: Maintain records including Name, Role, and Status (Active/Inactive) for seamless tracking.
  • ✅ Edit Employee Information: Update employee details like role changes, status updates, and profile modifications effortlessly.
  • ✅ Status Control (Block/Unblock): Instantly activate or deactivate employees based on their availability or company policies.
  • ✅ Delete Functionality: Remove outdated or irrelevant employee records securely to keep the system clutter-free.

Offer Management Description

Manage and create exciting offers seamlessly with our Offer Management System, designed to enhance customer engagement and drive sales. Each offer includes the following key details for maximum flexibility and reach

  • ✅ Offer Name: A clear and attractive name for the offer to grab attention and make it easily identifiable.
  • ✅ Promo Code: Assign a unique promo code to the offer, allowing customers to redeem it during checkout for discounts or rewards.
  • ✅ Select Items: Customize the offer by selecting specific items it applies to, giving you control over what’s included in the promotion.
  • ✅ Select Outlet: Target specific locations by assigning the offer to selected outlets, ensuring customers get relevant deals based on where they shop.
